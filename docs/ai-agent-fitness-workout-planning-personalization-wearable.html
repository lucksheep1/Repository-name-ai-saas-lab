The AI Agent Fitness Workout Problem

AI agents can now generate personalized workout plans, track fitness progress, and integrate with wearable devices through OpenAI Chat Completion and LangGraph. AI-powered fitness uses heart rate data, step counts, calories, and medical history to create periodized training plans for strength conditioning, cardio, and recovery optimization.
