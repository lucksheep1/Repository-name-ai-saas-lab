The AI Agent Interior Design Problem

AI agents can now assist with interior design and home decoration by generating photorealistic room designs, suggesting furniture placements, and creating personalized style recommendations. AI-powered interior design agents use diffusion models, multimodal LLMs, and AR integration to transform empty rooms into beautifully decorated spaces.
