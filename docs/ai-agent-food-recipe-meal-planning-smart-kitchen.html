The AI Agent Food Recipe Problem

AI agents can now generate personalized recipes, plan meals, and assist with smart kitchen management. AI-powered food apps use AWS Bedrock, RAG, and IoT integration to provide nutritional analysis, cooking guidance, and grocery management for dietary restrictions and smart kitchen IoT devices.
