The Autonomous Research Problem

Academic research is slow, fragmented, and expensive. You spend months reading papers, designing experiments, writing, and revising. Autonomous research agents change this: from a single idea, an AI agent can conduct literature review, run experiments, analyze results, and write a paper — with minimal human input. But the agent needs memory: research context, literature notes, experimental results, and revision history must persist across the entire research cycle.
