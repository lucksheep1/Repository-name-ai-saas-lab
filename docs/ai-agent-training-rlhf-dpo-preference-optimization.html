Training RLHF DPO

The AI Agent Training Problem

Training AI agents requires Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) and Direct Preference Optimization (DPO). Modern training frameworks use Ray and vLLM for distributed training, enabling production-ready agentic RL with PPO, DAPO, and other alignment techniques.
