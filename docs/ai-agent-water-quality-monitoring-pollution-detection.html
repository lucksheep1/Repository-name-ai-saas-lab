Water Pollution Monitoring

The AI Agent Water Quality Problem

AI agents can now monitor water quality, detect plastic pollution, and assess contamination in real-time. AI-powered water monitoring uses YOLOv8 object detection, neural ocean classification, and IoT sensor networks to provide pollution detection, wastewater treatment, and marine conservation for environmental agencies and researchers.
