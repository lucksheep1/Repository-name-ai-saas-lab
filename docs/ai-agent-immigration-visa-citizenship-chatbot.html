The AI Agent Immigration Visa Problem

AI agents can now assist with immigration visa applications, green card processing, H1B work permits, and citizenship naturalization. AI-powered immigration chatbots use LangChain and RAG to scrape USCIS content, answer queries, and recommend visa profiles for certification or denial based on ML analysis.
