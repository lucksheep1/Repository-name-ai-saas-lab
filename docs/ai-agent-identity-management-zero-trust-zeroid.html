The AI Agent Identity Management Problem

AI agents need proper identity management and zero-trust security frameworks. Autonomous Agent Identity Management Systems (AAIMS) provide OAuth/OIDC delegated authority where agents are distinct from users who authorize them, with policy enforcement and execution sandboxing.
