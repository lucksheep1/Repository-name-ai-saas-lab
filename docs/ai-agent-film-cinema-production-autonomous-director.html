The AI Agent Film Production Problem

AI agents can now act as autonomous film directors, screenwriters, and producers through multi-agent CoT planning and hierarchical reasoning. AI-powered cinema production uses script analysis, scene blocking, camera settings, and cinematography automation to generate multi-scene, multi-shot long-form videos with coherent narrative from text prompts or screenplay inputs.
