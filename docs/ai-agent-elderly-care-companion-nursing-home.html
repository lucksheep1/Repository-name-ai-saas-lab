The AI Agent Elderly Care Problem

AI agents can now provide daily monitoring for elderly care, chronic disease management, and companionship for nursing home residents. AI-powered elder care systems use dialogue-based interaction, multi-channel notifications, and connected devices to detect falls, remind medications, and support palliative care for diabetes and hypertension patients.
