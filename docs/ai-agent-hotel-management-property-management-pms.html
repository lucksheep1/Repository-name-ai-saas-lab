The AI Agent Hotel Management Problem

AI agents can now manage hotel properties, handle guest communications, and automate concierge services through WhatsApp-first and multilingual hospitality agents. AI-powered hotel PMS uses AutoGen, Streamlit, and Google ADK to provide conversational booking, room service, and channel manager integration for self-service accommodations and boutique hotels.
