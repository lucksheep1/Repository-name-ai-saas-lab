The AI Agent Pet Care Problem

AI agents can now monitor pet health, schedule vet appointments, and provide breed identification through computer vision and LLM chatbots. AI-powered pet care uses Odoo 17, Llama 2, and computer vision to offer personalized care advice, disease prediction, and breed recommendation for veterinary clinics, grooming salons, and pet owners.
