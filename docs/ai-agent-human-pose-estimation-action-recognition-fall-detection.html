The AI Agent Human Pose Estimation Problem

AI agents with computer vision capabilities now perform human pose estimation, action recognition, and skeleton tracking for applications ranging from elderly fall detection to physical therapy rehabilitation. These systems use IMU sensors, depth cameras, and advanced pose estimation models to monitor human activities in real-time.
