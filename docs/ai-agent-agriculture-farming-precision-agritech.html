The AI Agent Agriculture Problem

AI agents are transforming agriculture with precision farming, crop disease detection, and autonomous drones. Modern AI agriculture platforms use CNNs for health monitoring, geospatial ML for crop analysis, and drone technology for precision spraying to increase yield and reduce chemical usage.
