Social Simulation LLM Agents Emergence

The AI Agent Large-Scale Social Simulation Problem

AI agents can now simulate large-scale social behaviors and societies through LLM-driven agents. Large-scale social simulation enables researchers to study emergent social phenomena, opinion dynamics, and collective behaviors in virtual environments with millions of simulated agents.
