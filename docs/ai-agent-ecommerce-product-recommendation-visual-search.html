The AI Agent E-commerce Product Recommendation Problem

AI agents can now provide personalized product recommendations, visual search, and customer support for e-commerce platforms through RAG chatbots and transfer learning. AI-powered e-commerce uses PydanticAI, ResNet50, and cosine similarity to analyze visual features, recommend similar products, and automate order management for fashion and retail stores.
