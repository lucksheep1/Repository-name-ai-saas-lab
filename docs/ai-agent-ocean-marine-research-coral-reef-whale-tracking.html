Ocean Marine Research

The AI Agent Ocean Marine Research Problem

AI agents can now monitor coral reefs, track marine species, and detect microplastics through computer vision and environmental monitoring. AI-powered marine research uses underwater cameras, AI image recognition, and FAIR data principles to provide real-time analysis for marine biologists, oceanographers, and environmental scientists.
