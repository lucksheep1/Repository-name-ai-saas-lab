The AI Agent Real Estate Property Management Problem

AI agents can now manage real estate properties, handle lease negotiations, and automate landlord tasks through multi-agent architectures and voice AI. AI-powered property management uses RAG, LLMs, and scraping to provide maintenance image analysis, rent negotiation, tenant screening, and lease renewal automation for landlords and property managers.
