The AI Agent Safety Problem

As AI agents become more autonomous, understanding their decision-making becomes critical for safety. AI safety and interpretability research for agents covers mechanistic interpretability, transparency in agent trajectories, circuit-level analysis of transformer models, and methods for auditing agent behavior to ensure trustworthy AI systems.
