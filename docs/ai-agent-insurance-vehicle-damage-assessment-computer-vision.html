The AI Agent Insurance Vehicle Damage Problem

AI agents can now assess vehicle damage, process insurance claims, and estimate repair costs through computer vision and specialized multi-agent systems. AI-powered insurance uses CNN-based damage classification, VGG16 models, and semantic search to provide accident reconstruction, claims triage, and cost estimation for auto insurance companies, repair shops, and claims adjusters.
