Mental Health Ambient Sensing Digital Phenotyping

The AI Agent Mental Health Ambient Sensing Problem

AI agents can now passively monitor mental health through ambient sensing, using smartphone sensors, wearables, and ecological momentary assessment (EMA) to detect depression, anxiety, and mood changes in real-time. Digital phenotyping enables continuous mental health monitoring without active user input.

DepressionCenter/EMA-CleanR

"Efficient pre-processing, cleaning, and visualization of Ecological Momentary Assessment (EMA) survey data in R to enable high quality, real-time behavioral insights. Works to better characterize intraindividual variability in emotion and behavior in real-world contexts."— DepressionCenter/EMA-CleanR on GitHub
