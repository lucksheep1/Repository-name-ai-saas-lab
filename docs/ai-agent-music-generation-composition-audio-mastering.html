The AI Agent Music Generation Problem

AI agents can now compose music, generate lyrics, and produce audio through transformer and multimodal models. AI-powered music tools use LangGraph workflows, harmonix ensemble models, and SoTA TTS to provide song generation, music structure analysis, and audio mastering for musicians and content creators.
