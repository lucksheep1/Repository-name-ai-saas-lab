The AI Agent Autonomous Research Problem

Research is time-consuming — literature surveys take weeks, paper analysis is manual, writing is tedious. AI research agents automate the entire pipeline: from arXiv browsing to literature surveys to LaTeX paper generation. agent-memory stores research context, enabling persistent research sessions across days or weeks.

Why agent-memory Is the Research Context Memory

  • Research context persistence — remember papers read, hypotheses formed, experiments run
  • Long-term memory — research spans days/weeks, agent-memory's TTL handles this
  • memsearch-compatible — Markdown-first memory works alongside agent-memory
  • Literature survey memory — store survey results for quick retrieval
  • Experiment memory — remember experiment results for iterative research
  • MIT licensed — free for academic and commercial research
